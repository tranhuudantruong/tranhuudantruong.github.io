Lê Tuấn Đạt

The bookstore management system allows customers to perform functions such as search, order books, manage shopping carts, manage accounts and personal information easily and quickly. Besides, helping bookstores can easily perform functions such as direct purchase invoices, book import, book management, sales report statistics, product frequency.

To build a strong brand, one of the most important orientations of FAHASA is the human resource development strategy - the key to all success.

Eshop has more than 2,200 employees with good qualifications, enthusiasm, dynamism, and professionalism. The management team of Eshop has a long working life, is professional, has a lot of experience, can manage well and run the unit effectively.

Combining recruitment of qualified input human resources and a plan to foster knowledge, practice additional skills and prepare the successor team in the direction of regularity through short-term and long-term classes; The training courses for employees held in the country as well as abroad are paid special attention by FAHASA leaders and facilitate implementation. Therefore, the professional level of the staff is increasingly improved, meeting the increasing needs of the work as well as the development of society on the way to integration.
